Hank Hill


Born in: New York, New York 1956
Lives in: Arlen, Texas 1957-Present

Hank Hill is the assistant manager at Strickland Propane where he sells propane and propane accessories. He grew up in the small town known as Arlen, Texas and went to Arlen High School where he brought their football team to a state championship as starting Running Back. He still resides in Arlen with his wife Peggy Hill and their son Bobby Hill. Nowadays when Hank is not working, he likes to spend his time drinking beer with his friends in the driveway behind his house.

"I work for a living. And I mean real work. Not writing down gobbledygook. I provide people of this community with propane and propane accessories." -Hank Hill
