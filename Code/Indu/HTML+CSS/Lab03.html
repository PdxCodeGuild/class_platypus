WATER RESOURCES

With experience throughout all Water Divisions in Colorado as well as Texas, Arizona, Nebraska, and Utah – LRE is a recognized leader in water resources throughout the western United States. As the cornerstone for our firm’s expertise, our water resources group specializes in providing innovative solutions for water and land to meet the needs and goals of municipal, agricultural, federal and private clients in the areas of water supply planning, water rights, ground water, master planning, water reuse and design engineering.

Technology

Today, successful water resources management relies on an increasingly vast store of historical and real-time data. Our dedicated group of information specialists offer intelligent data integration know-how and can deliver processes and tools customized to meet each client’s very specific needs. Our team’s development experience ranges from custom desktop tools within the MS Office suite to open-source, web-based decision support systems backed by sophisticated SQL databases. Our combination of domain expertise and programming skills allows us to leverage technology to create analysis, communication and decision support tools that really fit with the challenges unique to this industry.
