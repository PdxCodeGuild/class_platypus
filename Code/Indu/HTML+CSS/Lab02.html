Ilayada

9/5/2018

Ilayada is a traditional Kerala sweet/snack which is made with rice flour/raw rice and coconut-jaggery filling. It is a very healthy and tasty dish which can be had as an evening snack. It is a traditional kerala evening snack; having authentic aroma and taste. Banana leaves, bay leaves, turmeric leaves etc.are used for making Ada It is prepared with very simple ingredients readily available in our kitchen. are used for making AdaDo try it and let me know how it is.

  • Riceflour -1 cup
  • Coconut -1 cup grated
  • Jaggery -3/4 cup
  • Cardamom -3
  • Salt pinch
  • Water as required

Method

  • Step 1:Take a large bowl and add rice flour and salt as required.
  • Step 2:Add warm water little by little to make a smooth dough.keep it aside.
  • Step 3:Take a saucepan,add grated coconut,jaggery,cardamom,(crushed) and 1/2 cup water
  • Cardamom -3
  • Step 4:Cook till the jaggery melts and water absorbs,it may take 5-6 minutes.then turn off the flame.
  • Step 5:Roll the rice in step 1 into a ball and flatten it onto the centre of a square piece of banana leaf. Place two tablespoons of the coconut-jaggery mix onto the flattened rice and fold the leaf and seal the edges.
  • Step 6:Steam this in an idli cooker for 15-20 minutes.
